A little bit about me ...

With a diverse background in law and auditing, I discovered my passion for software development and all things tech after completing the Software Developer Essentials bootcamp at iO Academy. This was a full-time 12-week bootcamp looking at some of the biggest programming languages with an emphasis on SOLID and design principles to ensure quality code that is scalable, maintainable and adheres to industry best practices.

Through project-based learning, we collaborated in teams to complete various projects, utilising Git version control, pair programming and agile methodologies such as Scrum and Kanban (Trello), simulating the dynamics of professional software development environments.
